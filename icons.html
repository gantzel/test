Icons
foundation.

The eCare icon set is designed by the YouSee team and covers navigation, actions, status and UI controls. All icons are 24 × 24 px and optimised for use on both light and dark surfaces.

Icons are available as PNG exports from Figma. Many come in a filled and an outlined variant. Three status icons — Check Circle, Info Circle and Warning Circle — are used in the Alert component.
